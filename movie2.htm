Casablanca - 5/5

Cast Reviews

Casablanca has been hailed as a masterpiece of cinema since it first appeared on the silver screen. Featuring powerhouse actors Humphrey Bogart and Ingrid Bergman and set in Casablanca, Morocco in the year of 1941 the film centers around character Rick Blaine (Bogart). Rick owns a popular nightclub in town, and while the French still maintain control they answer to the Third Reich. Trouble finds Rick in the form of two letters of transit, which would allow anyone to get out of Nazi occupied territory, and an old lover by the name of Ilsa(Bergman). Ilsa is married to a resistance leader who needs to get out of the country, but the Nazis have him under their watch constantly. As Rick tries to stay out of the situation he finds himself drawn deeper into it. Plagued by memories, nazis, police, and his conscience there's no shortage of intrigue as one man caught in the middle tries to maintain his neutrality.
