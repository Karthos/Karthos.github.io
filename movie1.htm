Singin' in the Rain - 5/5

Cast Reviews

Few public figures are as loved and as entertaining as Gene Kelly, singer, dancer, actor, director and star of Singin' in the Rain. An classic of the musical genre, Singin' in the Rain takes viewers back to the tail end of the roaring twenties where Don Lockwood(Kelly), a stuntman turned actor is working on a film at the time when talking pictures were brand new. Joined by friends Cosmo Brown(O'Connor) and Kathy Selden(Reynolds), Lockwood and the Monumental Film studio crew try to turn a disaster into a smash hit. Full of memorable musical numbers watch as Gene Kelly and others dance, laugh, sing, fall in love, and hit a few roadbumps on their way to success. A classic fit for the whole family, don't get caught out in the rain, sing in it!
