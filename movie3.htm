Gone with the Wind - 4/5

Cast Reviews

This masterpiece of American cinema from director Victor Fleming and writer Margaret Mitchell is a tale of life in the old South. Scarlett O'hara, played by Vivien Leigh, comes from a prominent southern family. Life is good for her, except she cannot have the man she wants. Follow Scarlett through her trials and tribulations through war, destruction of her family home, post war reconstruction, and marriage to Rhett Butler, played by Clark Gable, one of the most famous faces of the silver screen. Enduring all sorts of hardships, even the burning of Atlanta, Scarlett will never give up in pursuit of the life she wants. Winner of 10 academy awards complimented by a renowned soundtrack, this classic is one you're sure to love. Now fully remastered in color, be sure to see Gone With The Wind before it's gone from the screens.
